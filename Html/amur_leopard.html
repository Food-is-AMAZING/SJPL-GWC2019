Amur Leopard

Since 1996, the Amur Leopard has been classified by the IUCN (International Union for Conservation of Nature) as Critically Endangered, with less than 70 individuals thought to exist today. It is hunted and killed for its beautiful fur, its habitat is being destroyed for human settlement and agriculture.
